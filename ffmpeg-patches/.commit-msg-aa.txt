feat(aa): add vf_pelorus_aa_vulkan (anime warp-AA + line-darkening, Vulkan)

A single-pass GPU port of awarpsharp2 (warp anti-aliasing) plus an optional
FastLineDarken-style line-darkening pass. Anime line-art accumulates aliasing
through repeated scale/encode; awarpsharp builds a blurred edge-strength map and
warps each pixel toward the nearest edge by the gradient of that map, pulling
stair-stepped samples onto the line (anti-aliasing + thinning) without the
ringing a sharpen would add. Line-darkening deepens the dark side of edges so
lines stay crisp. Luma only; chroma passes through. Zero-copy in VRAM
(FF_VK_REP_FLOAT UNORM, bit-depth-agnostic).

Pure pixel transform — does NOT link libpelorus, so registration is deps-only
(no require_pkg_config). Second filter of the anime `tune` pipeline. The
reference shader libpelorus/shaders/pelorus_aa.comp and the inline GLSL are kept
in lockstep.

AVOptions: blur (edge-map radius), depth (warp scale), thresh (edge clamp),
darkstr (line-darkening), edge (line threshold), planes, fast (opt-in shared-
memory hoist of the redundant sobel-mag, ADR-0140; bit-identical, a large
ALU-bound speedup on every GPU).

ADR: docs/adr/0124-anime-aa.md
