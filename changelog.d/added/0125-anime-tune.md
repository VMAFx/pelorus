- **Anime tune** (docs capstone): a recommended GPU pre-encode pipeline for animation that composes the existing filters — `pelorus_analyze_vulkan roi=1` (auto-detected flat-ROI) + `pelorus_dehalo_vulkan` (ring/halo removal) + `pelorus_aa_vulkan` (warp anti-aliasing + line-darkening) + `pelorus_deband_vulkan` (smart deband) — at 10-bit, then steers the encoder's bits onto the flats with `-pelorus_roi 1` under constant-QP. Each leg targets a distinct anime artefact (banding, starved flats, ringing, jaggies); presented as a documented, retunable chain rather than a new meta-filter (the filters are independent and composable). The per-leg ROI wins are measured (−47%/−41% banding); the composed end-to-end SSIMULACRA2 + CAMBI proof is the documented follow-up. New guide [docs/usage/anime.md](docs/usage/anime.md) (ADR-0125).
